MCL38

MCL38 introduces innovative design elements developed in the
previous model, MCL60 in particular, the floor design and
aerodynamic performance Improved track performance.

This led to McLaren performing better in the second half of the season,
Expectations are rising for the 2024 season.

Transmission

McLaren 8-speed + 1 reverse
sequential seamless semi-
automatic transmission
operated via paddle shifters with
limited slip friction clutch
epicyclic differential.

Cockpit

Cockpit survival cell
incorporating impact resistant
construction and
anti-penetration panels.

Engine

Mercedes-AMG F1 M14 E
Performance
1.6 L (98 cu in) direct injection V6
turbocharged engine limited to
15,000 RPM in a mid-mounted,
rear-wheel drive layout.

Tyers

bringing our total to six

select three for each event.

suspension legs, pullrod
operating inboard torsion bar,
spring and damper system.

wing with driver-operated drag
reduction system.
